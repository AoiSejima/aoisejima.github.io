AOI SEJIMA

  • WORKS

  • TRUST

  • Designed

    March 2021

  • Medium

    inDesign, Photoshop

  • OBJECTIVE

    Conduct an interview and design a book.

    CONCEPT

    I conducted an interview with my roommate, where I displayed the friendship and bonding we achieved despite some of the political situations our national countries go through.

  • INSPIRATION

    Blue represents my roommate from South Korea and pink depict me from Japan.
    To display our honesty towards each other, I utilized the raw binding technique and the translucent paper for the cover page. The two colors slowly emerge throughout the book to represent how the relationship and our emotions shifted over time.
