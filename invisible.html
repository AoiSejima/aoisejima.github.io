    Stories as Networks:

    Getting inspired by In Praise of Shadow by Jun’ichiō Tanizaki, I designed and coded this in regard to presenting the importance of the light and shadow relationship utilized in Japanese architecture.

I used Cormorant font to execute the humble, sophisticated, and delicate Japanese design.

EAEVES

Enter other rooms from here. The white font displays the brightness of a room under the roof.

VERANDA

The leading gradually gets tighter and font-weight gets thicker. Inside of the room looks darker when the outside is bright.

ALCOVE

The bar shifts the color of the background and font. Shadow of an object in a room darkens and disappears in the dark when no light comes in.

SHOJI

Shoji moves around with the cursor. The font color-changing shows how the paper absorbs and weakens brightness.

GOLD

Hovering on the textbox changes the scale. Distance shifts the brightness of gold.
