  • Problem

    Fear of going out and walking alone at night
    Crimes targetting specific demographics have been increasing since COVID-19.
    Participating agencies on 2020 FBI HATE CRIMES STATISTICS reorted 5,227 race, ethnicity, ancestry-based incidents in 2020, (32% increase from 2019).

  • 2,871 anti-Black / African American (49% increase since 2019)
  • 279 anti-Asian (77% increase since 2019)
  • 517 anti-Hispanic / Latino
  • 869 anti-White

    Solution

    An app with a map that indicates safer street to walk.
    Mitigate the fear of walking outside and report as soon as possible if you experience / witness a crime.


    TARGET USER

    Target of hate crimes


    INSPIRATION

    Contribute to STOP AAPI HATE (Asian American Pacific Islander) organization, and make an app version of their website to make it accessible for the users in an emergency situation. Logo and color scheme is maintained from the organization's website theme.

Highlight

Embedded a map that will display illustrative icons to notify users which streets they should avoid walking.

Blue Flash Light Icon is a sign of patrol taking place.
Red Explanation Triangle Icon is the unprotected/unsanitary streets with broken street lights, overflowing garbage cans, and abandoned buildings. The racism crimes and violence has a tendency to occur on the street or subway that are not protected by the police or environmentally maintained.
Attack (Strick, Knife, Gunshot) Icons symbolize past crimes occured on the streets.
