  • Intro

    Contributing to the organization called STOP AAPI HATE, aka Asian American Pacific Islander, I made an application version of their website that lets users report incidents, donate, and learn about incidents and resources around them.

    Problem

    Crimes targetting Asian women and elderies increased significantly after COVID-19. The stickers of the past crimes on the map will let users know where they should avoid walking.

Action Week: Contribute to an Organization

Maintaining their theme, I added a map feature with illustrative icons that display what kind of attacks have occurred in which specific location. The blue flash light icon is a sign of where a patrol is taking place, and the red explanation triangle icon informs the unprotected/unsanitary streets with broken street lights, overflowing garbage cans, and abandoned buildings as these racism crimes and violence has a tendency to occur on the street or subway that are not protected by the police or environmentally maintained. Opening up an application on your phone is much quicker and easier in an emergency situation to reach out. I consider that this application will benefit both the community and the organization, and this could inform the government or police officers where they need to pay attention to more for environmental justice.
