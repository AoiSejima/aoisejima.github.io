  • INSPIRATION

    Getting inspired by In Praise of Shadow by Junichio Tanizaki, I designed and coded this in regard to presenting the importance of the light and shadow relationship utilized in Japanese architecture.

    I used Cormorant font to execute the humble, sophisticated, and delicate Japanese design.

EAEVES

Entrance of a house.
The white font displays the brightness of a room under the roof.

VERANDA

Inside of the room looks darker when the outside is bright. Leading gradually gets tighter as font-weight gets thicker.

ALCOVE

Shadow of an object in a room darkens and disappears in the dark when no light comes in. The bar shifts color of the background and font.

SHOJI

Shoji paper absorbs and weakens sunlight coming in from outside. Font color changes color as shoji moves around.

GOLD

Distance shifts the brightness of gold. Hovering on the textbox changes the scale of the text box.
